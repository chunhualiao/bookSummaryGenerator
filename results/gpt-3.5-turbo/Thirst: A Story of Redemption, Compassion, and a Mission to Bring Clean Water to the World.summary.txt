"Thirst: A Story of Redemption, Compassion, and a Mission to Bring Clean Water to the World" by Scott Harrison is a powerful memoir that details the author's journey from a life of excess and selfishness to one of purpose and service. Here are the top 10 main points of the book:

1. **Personal Transformation**: Scott Harrison begins the book by recounting his early years as a nightclub promoter living a hedonistic lifestyle filled with drugs and alcohol. He describes his realization that his life lacked meaning and purpose, leading him to seek redemption and make a change.

2. **Founding Charity: Water**: Harrison shares the story of how he founded Charity: Water, a non-profit organization dedicated to providing clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. He explains the inspiration behind starting the organization and the challenges he faced in the early stages.

3. **Impact of Clean Water**: The book highlights the transformative impact that access to clean water can have on communities in need. Harrison shares stories of individuals whose lives have been changed by the simple act of having access to clean water, emphasizing the importance of this basic human need.

4. **Fundraising Challenges**: Harrison discusses the difficulties he encountered in raising funds for Charity: Water and the creative strategies he employed to attract donors and supporters. He emphasizes the importance of transparency and accountability in the non-profit sector.

5. **Innovative Approaches**: The author describes the innovative approaches Charity: Water has taken to fund its projects, including leveraging technology and social media to engage donors and raise awareness. He also discusses the organization's commitment to ensuring that 100% of public donations go directly to funding water projects.

6. **Field Work**: Harrison recounts his experiences visiting communities around the world that have benefitted from Charity: Water's projects. He shares poignant stories of individuals he has met and the impact that access to clean water has had on their lives.

7. **Challenges and Setbacks**: The book delves into the challenges and setbacks Harrison has faced in his journey to bring clean water to the world. He discusses the complexities of working in developing countries and the need to navigate cultural, logistical, and political obstacles.

8. **Global Water Crisis**: Harrison sheds light on the global water crisis and the staggering statistics related to lack of access to clean water and sanitation. He underscores the urgency of addressing this issue and the importance of collective action to make a difference.

9. **Lessons Learned**: Throughout the book, Harrison reflects on the lessons he has learned through his experiences with Charity: Water. He emphasizes the power of compassion, empathy, and perseverance in driving positive change and making a meaningful impact in the world.

10. **Call to Action**: In the final chapters, Harrison issues a call to action for readers to get involved in addressing the global water crisis. He encourages individuals to support organizations working to provide clean water to those in need and to make a difference in their own communities.

Overall, "Thirst" is a compelling and inspiring account of one man's journey to redemption and his mission to bring clean water to the world. Through personal anecdotes, powerful storytelling, and a call to action, Scott Harrison invites readers to join him in making a positive impact and transforming lives through the gift of clean water.
