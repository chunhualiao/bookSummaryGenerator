Certainly! Here are the top 10 main points from the book "What It Takes: Lessons in the Pursuit of Excellence" by Stephen A. Schwarzman:

1. **Importance of Ambition**: Schwarzman emphasizes the significance of ambition in achieving success. He believes that ambition is the driving force behind accomplishing great things and encourages readers to set ambitious goals.

2. **Persistence and Resilience**: The author stresses the importance of persistence and resilience in the face of challenges and setbacks. Schwarzman shares personal anecdotes where he encountered failures but persisted with determination to overcome them.

3. **Building Relationships**: Schwarzman highlights the value of building strong relationships in both personal and professional life. He emphasizes the importance of networking, fostering connections, and nurturing relationships to achieve success.

4. **Leadership Skills**: The book discusses essential leadership qualities such as decisiveness, strategic thinking, and effective communication. Schwarzman shares insights on how to inspire and lead teams towards a common goal.

5. **Risk-Taking**: Schwarzman encourages readers to take calculated risks and embrace opportunities for growth. He shares his own experiences of taking risks in business and the rewards that came with those decisions.

6. **Adaptability**: The author emphasizes the need to be adaptable in a rapidly changing world. Schwarzman discusses the importance of being flexible, open to new ideas, and willing to pivot when necessary.

7. **Continuous Learning**: Schwarzman advocates for lifelong learning and personal development. He believes that continuous education and self-improvement are essential for staying ahead in a competitive environment.

8. **Financial Acumen**: The book delves into the importance of financial literacy and understanding the principles of finance. Schwarzman shares insights on managing finances wisely and making informed investment decisions.

9. **Integrity and Ethics**: Schwarzman emphasizes the significance of integrity and ethical behavior in all aspects of life. He discusses the importance of upholding moral values and conducting oneself with honesty and transparency.

10. **Legacy and Giving Back**: The author touches upon the idea of leaving a legacy and giving back to society. Schwarzman discusses the importance of philanthropy and making a positive impact on the world beyond personal success.

In "What It Takes: Lessons in the Pursuit of Excellence," Stephen A. Schwarzman provides valuable insights and practical advice on achieving success, leadership, and personal growth. Through his own experiences and lessons learned, Schwarzman offers a roadmap for individuals striving for excellence in their endeavors.
